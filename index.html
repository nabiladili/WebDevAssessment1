GEOGRAPHY

The Kingdom of Bahrain is located to the East of Saudi Arabia, it is a relatively small island with an area of around 770 kilometers squared. It is completely surrounded by water, however the King Fahad Causeway is a bridge that links Bahrain to Saudi Arabia. Bahrain is surrounded by several smaller islands that are connected by amazing bridges. However, there are a few islands a little further off, that you can only reach by boat! Which makes for an amazing weekend adventure! Keep reading to find out the numerous cities that we have here!

FOUR FUN FACTS ABOUT BAHRAIN!

Bahrain is filled with numerous possibilities for adventure, it also has several interesting and surprising facts. Here are four fun facts about the Kingdom of Bahrain!

  1. Bahrain is only 46 years old!
  2. It was one of the earliest countries to become a fully Islamic country.
  3. It was the first ever country to host the Grand Prix in the Middle East!
  4. It is loaded with archeological finds!
