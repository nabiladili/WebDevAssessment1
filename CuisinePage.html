BAHRAIN HAS MANY FAMOUS DELICIOUS FOODS!

SAMBOOSA

A thin baked good, stuffed with diverse choices of fillings and formed into a triangle. Stuffing can include other alternatives such as cheese, meat and pizza-style fillings. Fried to a superbly crunchy brilliant pastry.

MEHYAWA

Mehyawa is made by fermenting anchovies over a few days to create a unique flavor that is mixed with anise, coriander, and cumin. Then it is blended to create a thick texture that would be poured over other dishes. It is usually used as a sauce.

LUQAIMAT

A sweet delicacy usually served during Ramadan at the breaking of the fast, made with flour, yeast, sugar, saffron, and some cardamom, these crunchy gold desserts are traditional at almost every Bahraini table.

MACHBOOS

A rice based dish that is made with either chicken or meat, and sometimes shrimps. The use of spices and dried lime is essential for this dish. The rice and meats (chicken, lamb, beef or shrimp) are cooked together, giving the rice more flavoring. The rice is also garnished with rose water and saffron and served with a a green chili sauce (Daqoos).
